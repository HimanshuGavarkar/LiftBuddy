Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about LiftBuddy.

General

What is LiftBuddy?
LiftBuddy is a community-driven platform that connects commuters for shared rides. We help students and daily commuters reduce carbon emissions, save money, and build connections by carpooling.
Is LiftBuddy free to use?
Yes! LiftBuddy is completely free to join and use. We believe sustainable travel should be accessible to everyone. There are no hidden fees or subscriptions.
How is LiftBuddy different from other ride-sharing apps?
LiftBuddy is a community movement, not a commercial service. We focus on connecting regular commuters for daily rides, with a strong emphasis on environmental impact and community building.

Safety

How do you verify users?
All users go through a verification process including phone number verification and optional ID verification. We also have a rating system where users can review each other after rides.
What safety features does LiftBuddy have?
We offer live ride tracking, emergency SOS button, trusted contacts sharing, in-app chat (no need to share phone numbers), and a comprehensive reporting system.

Rides

How do I find a ride?
Simply enter your pickup and drop-off locations, select your preferred time, and browse available rides. You can also set up recurring ride alerts for your daily commute.
How do I offer a ride?
Post your route, departure time, and number of available seats. Interested riders will send you requests, and you can choose who to accept based on their profile and ratings.
What if I need to cancel a ride?
You can cancel a ride through the app. We encourage cancelling as early as possible to give others time to find alternatives. Frequent last-minute cancellations may affect your rating.
