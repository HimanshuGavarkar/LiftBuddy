Safety Tips for Riders

  • Check the driver's profile

    Review ratings, reviews, and verification status before accepting a ride.

  • Share your trip details

    Let a friend or family member know your ride details ‚Äî driver info, route, and expected arrival time.

  • Verify the vehicle

    Before getting in, confirm the car's make, model, and licence plate match what's shown in the app.

  • Sit in the back seat

    Whenever possible, sit in the back for more personal space and easy exit access.

  • Trust your instincts

    If something feels wrong, don't hesitate to cancel the ride or ask to be dropped off.

Safety Tips for Drivers

  • Verify your passengers

    Check the rider's profile and confirm their name before starting the trip.

  • Keep your vehicle maintained

    Ensure your car is in good condition with valid insurance and registration.

  • Follow traffic rules

    Always drive safely, follow speed limits, and avoid distractions.

  • Set clear expectations

    Communicate about music, AC, stops, and other preferences upfront.
