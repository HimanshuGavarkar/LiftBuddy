About LiftBuddy

A people-led initiative building greener cities through shared mobility.

Our Story

LiftBuddy started as a simple idea among college students in 2024 ‚Äî what if we could share our daily commutes and make a real impact on the environment?

Every day, millions of empty car seats travel across India. We saw an opportunity to connect commuters heading the same way, reducing both carbon emissions and travel costs.

Today, LiftBuddy has grown into a movement of eco-conscious travellers committed to building cleaner, greener cities ‚Äî one shared ride at a time.

Our Mission

We're on a mission to transform urban mobility by making ride-sharing the natural choice for daily commuters.

This is not about profit ‚Äî it's about people, planet, and progress. We believe that collective action can solve the climate crisis, one shared ride at a time.

By connecting students, professionals, and everyday commuters, we're building a community that proves sustainable travel can be convenient, affordable, and social.
